Kings Beach

Located at the Northern end of Lake Tahoe, Kings Beach is a fantastic place to visit.

























Sand Harbor

Sand Harbor is located on the north eastern edge of the lake. With amazing views, boat launch sites, and warm weather, Sand Harbor is the place to be.

























Emerald Bay

Emerald Bay is one of the most photographed places in Lake Tahoe. The island in the middle is known as Fannette Island.
